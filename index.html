My favorite Band.

Cold play

Coldplay is an English rock band formed in London in the mid 90s and has managed to sell over 100 million records worldwide. In the 2000s they were voted the fourth best artists by rolling stone readers.

The band is composed of five members

    Chris Martin

    Jonny Buckland

    Guy Berryman

    Will Champion

    Phil Harvey

Coldplay have produced many hits over the years with most of the songs remaining timeless no matter the season. Here is a list of my all time favorites.

    Viva la vida

    Yellow

    In my place

    Amsterdam

    Fix you

    Shiver
